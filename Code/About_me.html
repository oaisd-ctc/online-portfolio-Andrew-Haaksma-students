Accomplishment

In this picture, I am skiing in Colorado where I like to spend my time skiing through the trees and meeting new people. With that, I don’t like skiing in Colorado but anywhere, because going to new places is always so much fun. The reason is that doing stuff that gets my heart pumping gives me the biggest enjoyment in the world. That is why I also like mountain biking, water sports and so much more, because a life with no excitement is just no fun.

When doing different activities they can sometimes be a rinse and repeat so I like to spice things up. So I decided to do some paragliding in the Swiss Alps. Where we got to stay in a valley surrounded by mountains (quick tip most people there speak English) where we could first try this but doing fun activities isn’t the only thing I like doing. One thing that me and my whole family do is that we are not allowed to go to any restaurants that we could just go to in America. Food options are limited to the cuisine of the area though I can be a bit of a picky eater. I will suck it up and try it but honestly, it is sometimes the best food I have eaten.

One of my biggest hobbies is to learn about the Renaissance, learn about mythology, dnd, and go to Renaissance fairs. I don’t exactly know why I love to do this stuff but it always fascinates me on how creative people were. Like with creature designs examples like the dragon or unicorn and the stories they told about the Greeks give me a frill of excitement almost like I was a little kid reading a bedtime story. With that, I love going to Renaissance fairs because one thing that they do there is that things are handmade. So not machines made through copying and pasting but hand-knitted clothing melted down steal into different things and so much more. So if you decide to go to a Renaissance fair make sure to have money because they do charge for the time they put into the item. Although it is a price hike, the food there can be some of the most delicious things, especially their pie shakes yum (don’t ask me why but it is there and that does make me happy). That is why I love studying this stuff as much as I do.
